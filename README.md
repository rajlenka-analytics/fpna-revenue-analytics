# FP&A & Revenue Analytics Portfolio

This repository showcases practical, business-focused projects in Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A), Revenue Analytics, Unit Economics, and Decision Support. The work reflects real-world scenarios commonly encountered in product, mobility, fintech, and enterprise environments.

The projects emphasize clarity of financial thinking, structured analysis, and management-ready insights rather than academic or theoretical models.

---

## Projects Overview

### 1. Marketplace Revenue & Unit Economics Analysis
- City-wise and segment-wise revenue analysis
- Incentive efficiency and contribution margin evaluation
- Identification of margin leakage and diminishing returns on incentives
- Focus on unit economics and operational drivers impacting profitability

**Key Skills Demonstrated:**  
Revenue Analytics, Unit Economics, Contribution Margin, Business Insights

---

### 2. FP&A – Forecast vs Actual & Variance Analysis
- Monthly forecasting of revenue and variable costs

- Structured management commentary explaining performance movements

FP&A, Forecasting, Variance Analysis, Financial Storytelling

P&L Analysis, Financial Commentary, Business Partnering

SQL, Data Analysis, Self-Serve Analytics for Finance
